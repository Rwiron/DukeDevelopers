Joseph G Dukuly - CEO

Joseph Dukuly leads Duke Developers as its Chief Executive Officer. He brings a unique blend of public and private sector experience, having served as Special Assistant to the Representative of District #12 in Liberia's House of Representatives. Joseph holds an MBA from Rome Business School. With his expertise in development and infrastructure, he drives Duke Developers' mission to deliver innovative and sustainable solutions, transforming communities and empowering lives..

Accomplished data analyst and visionary social entrepreneur focused on innovative solutions for Sub-Saharan Africa. Founder of Kwado Energy, increasing access to clean cooking fuel through bioethanol dispensers. Google-certified data analyst proficient in SQL, Tableau, and R.

Key achievements include:

  • Developed web-to-text job matching platform connecting employers with low-wage job seekers
  • Pioneered RoboGarden coding literacy program for K-12 students in Rwanda and Ghana
  • 15 years of experience driving social impact initiatives
  • Expertise in data analysis, social entrepreneurship, and technological solutions

Dedicated to formalizing Africa's informal sector and expanding access to emerging technologies globally through institutional partnerships.
