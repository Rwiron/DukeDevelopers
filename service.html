Frequently Asked Questions

What You Need to Know

What types of construction projects do you handle?
We specialize in general construction projects, including residential, commercial, and industrial developments, ensuring high-quality standards and timely delivery.
Do you offer project management services?
Yes, our experienced project managers oversee projects from planning to execution, ensuring efficient resource allocation and risk management.
Can you assist with renewable energy solutions?
Absolutely. We provide innovative renewable energy solutions, including solar and wind power installations, to promote sustainability and reduce environmental impact.
What IT services do you offer?
Our IT services include software development, IT consulting, cybersecurity, data analytics, and cloud computing solutions tailored to your business needs.
Do you supply electrical equipment?
Yes, we supply a variety of electrical equipment, including smart meters, transformers, safety gear, and cables, ensuring reliable and efficient distribution systems.
How do you ensure quality in your projects?
Quality is at the core of everything we do. We follow strict standards, use top-quality materials, and have skilled professionals managing every aspect of your project.
Can you assist with property management?
Yes, we offer comprehensive property management services, including maintenance, renovations, tenant management, and more.
Do you offer support for security systems?
Yes, we specialize in installing and managing security systems, including surveillance cameras and traffic management systems.
Are your services available internationally?
Yes, Duke Developers operates in Liberia, the USA, Canada, Rwanda, and Europe, delivering world-class solutions to meet global standards.
How can I get started with your services?
You can contact us via phone, email, or our website to discuss your project requirements and receive a customized proposal.
